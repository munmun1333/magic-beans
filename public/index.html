MAGIC BEANS

Online multiplayer domino beanstalk game

How to Play

• Build the tallest beanstalk by matching domino ends
• After each play, auto-draw one tile from the pile
• Magic Beans (doubles) = extra turn + steal from opponent!
• Flying Dutchman (double 6) = everyone salutes!
• Can't play? Draw one tile. No match = turn passes
• 6 on top + 6 in hand = chain play!
• Most pips on beanstalk wins!
