MAGIC BEANS

A domino beanstalk game

How to Play

• Build the longest beanstalk by matching domino ends
• After each play, draw one tile from the pile
• Magic Beans (doubles) give you an extra turn!
• Flying Dutchman (double 6) — everyone must salute!
• Can't play? Draw from pile until you can (or pile is empty)
• Game ends when all players are stuck. Most pips wins!
